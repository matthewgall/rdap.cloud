Frequently Asked Questions

What does rdap.cloud return?

rdap.cloud aggregates RDAP responses from authoritative servers and falls back to WHOIS only when needed. We normalize responses so they are consistent and predictable.

Does this replace RDAP servers?

No. We proxy and cache responses so you do not have to maintain a full bootstrap client or repeatedly query upstream servers.

Why is a lookup returning an error?

The target may be invalid or unsupported by RDAP. We include a clear error message in the response. If you see an issue, open a GitHub issue.

How often are bootstrap files updated?

Bootstrap data is cached and refreshed periodically to stay current while minimizing upstream traffic.

Is there rate limiting?

Yes. We apply fair rate limits to protect upstreams and keep the service stable.
